Goldenhand

Author: Garth Nix

Genre: Fantasy

Year Published: 2016

Goldenhand is the fourth book in the Abhorsen series by Garth Nix, following the journey of Lirael and the Abhorsen-in-Waiting, Sameth. Together, they must face the rising threat of the Dead who are invading the Old Kingdom, aiming to take control of the Charter and bring ruin to the world.
