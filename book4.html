Twilight

Author: Stephenie Meyer

Genre: Paranormal Romance, Fantasy

Year Published: 2005

Twilight is a captivating story of a teenage girl, Bella Swan, who falls in love with Edward Cullen, a vampire. Set in the misty town of Forks, Washington, Bella is drawn into the supernatural world, as she discovers the truth behind Edward’s existence, leading to a tumultuous relationship filled with danger, love, and sacrifice.
